Project Overview

The Fitness & Physiotherapy Exercise Planner is a web application designed to help users manage and track their therapeutic exercises. Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing chronic pain, or simply looking to improve your flexibility and strength, this tool provides structured guidance for your exercise routine.

Key Features

  • Browse exercises organized by category (Back Pain, Knee Pain, Stretching, Muscle Strength)
  • View detailed step-by-step instructions for each exercise
  • Built-in timer to guide your exercise duration
  • Track your progress and completed exercises
  • Accessible design for all users
  • Mobile-responsive layout for exercise anywhere
